Hikari Hajime is a kind hearted half-elf woman. She was born between a dark elf mother and a half elf father. She was kind since she was a child and in her hometown is known as one of the most beautiful woman in the entire town, as well as being the kindest on top of that. She often gets hit on and punishes those who take it too far. Her motherly nature earned her the nickname Mana Mother. Her adeptness with magic was second to nearly none. Her high compatabilty for support magic as well as befriending animals and beasts thought untamable gave her a large prestiege. The second most well known member of the Bakckup Hero party, and the one viewed in the most positive light. “Don't worry hun, I'll take good care of you. Mom is here, I'll keep you safe.”


Physical Description on Hero

Hikari Hajime is a young elven woman with deep brown skin and long, flowing violet hair tied in a high ponytail. Her large green eyes reflect a confident and warm demeanor. She has sharp, pointed ears and elegant facial features characteristic of her elven heritage.

She wears a dark violet tunic and skirt combination, cinched with a tan leather belt for a sleek and mobile silhouette. A deep purple cloak, fastened by a gold clasp at her collar, adds a regal flair. Her outfit is practical yet refined, designed for ease of movement and magical focus.

Hikari carries no visible weapons, relying instead on her powerful magic and the companionship of her Drake. She wears leather arm guards and sturdy boots, subtly enchanted for protection.

Hikari has a slender but athletic build, emphasizing her grace and agility over brute strength. Her long limbs and upright posture give her an elegant presence. Her hair and cloak often catch the wind, enhancing his ethereal, otherworldly appearance.
