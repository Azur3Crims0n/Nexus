Physical Description on Hero

He's an average sized young man who wears a persistant bright, friendly expression. His hair is hazel brown and always well kept, making his friendly aura even more inviting.
He wears a simple shirt and pants you can find just about anywhere, keeping his looks on the more average side of the spectrum. His light and minimal armor covered the very basics and didn't look all that uncomfortable, keeping his simplistic look quite uniform. Keeping his bow in hand and readied, his spell prepared as the light refracts off and casts shadows over the contours of his body, his eyes staying vigilent for danger at all times - he prepared himself for battle.
