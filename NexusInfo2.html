Takumi Fritz is a young male assasin-archer-mage with a lean, agile build suited for speed and precision. He has short, tousled brown hair that frames his face and intense, determined brown eyes. His expression often reflects focus and resolve, even in the heat of battle.

He wears a forest-green cloak that drapes over his shoulders, blending well with woodland surroundings. Beneath it, he sports a fitted tan tunic and dark brown trousers designed for mobility. Sturdy leather gloves and a wide utility belt complete his practical adventurer's

Takumi wields a finely crafted wooden longbow, designed for both distance and accuracy. He carries a round wooden shield reinforced with a steel rim and boss for added defense. His arrows are tipped with a potion coat, suggesting that he uses poison and potion effects in combat.

Takumi stands at an average height with a lean, wiry frame built for speed and endurance. His posture is upright and balanced, reflecting disciplined training. Small scars on his hands and forearms hint at countless hours spent in archery and combat practice.
