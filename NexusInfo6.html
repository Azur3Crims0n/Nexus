Physical Description on Villain

Clad in a crimson coat with baroque patterns and a wide-brimmed black hat, Sound Bomb walks the line between outlaw and legend. His glowing red eyes betray a supernatural edge, hinting at powers beyond mortal ken. A golden chain hangs from his neck, a symbol of defiance worn like a badge of honor. His left hand bears a bold tattoo: an anarchist emblem and the word “ANARCHY,” declaring his philosophy without a whisper.
A guitar slung over his shoulder is more than an instrument—it’s a weapon, a voice, and a battle cry. Whether stirring revolt through rebellious melodies or facing foes with a charismatic smirk, Sound Bomb is as dangerous as he is magnetic. No one knows where he came from, but when he arrives, order trembles—and the people remember the song.
