Physical Description on Hero

Naomi Hinode has long, flowing lavender hair and piercing violet eyes, often framed by a calm yet intense expression that hints at a storm beneath the surface.

She wears a sleek, black tactical outfit reinforced with light armor, designed for both mobility and protection, with dual belts and gloves suited for combat efficiency.

Naomi wields a Kusarigama crackling with lightning energy, which coils around her with an almost sentient menace, emphasizing her command over both precision and chaos.

Surrounded by arcs of lightning and a shroud of determination, Naomi exudes a commanding, enigmatic aura that unnerves foes and inspires allies
